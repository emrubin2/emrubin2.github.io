Hello I'm Evan!
I'm a cartograher, GIS professional, and foodie. I'm originally from Lancaster County and now currently live in Maryland and work for the Aberdeen Proving Grounds as a GIS Specialist. Before this I worked as a GIS Technician in Princeton, NJ for ALK Technologies

Back to the Beginning
My interest in GIS and computer science began in 2005 when I discovered Google Street View during a study hall in 8th grade. Since then, I've been very interested in Geography and travel! In 2010, I joined the Pennsylvania Army National Guard and also enrolled at Millersville University of Pennsylvania. In 2015, I graduated with a degree in geospatial applications and moved to New Jersey to begin my career in GIS. I was honorably discharged from the National Guard in 2016.

State of the Union
I am currently enrolled at the University of Wisconsin Madison studying GIS and Web Map Programming. I'm interested in spatial web and mobile programming, interactive cartography, and GIS tool development specifically for ESRI ArcGIS for Desktop 10.
